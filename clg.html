ABOUT US

At RACE beautiful campus, you’ll find a diverse and welcoming community that will teach you life skills along with having fun.Founded in 2014, Race women's Degree College is a distinguished institution located in the state of Hyderabad. The institute offers a range of Degree courses, which include UG. These courses are designed to be delivered in full-time mode. The institute offers various courses such as B.Com, B.Sc., BBA covering major streams such as Accounting and Commerce, Business and Management Studies. The institute have 350 seats and it provides quality education at an affordable fee, making these courses accessible to all, and allowing students to acquire knowledge and expertise in their chosen fields. Moreover, the institute provides excellent infrastructure facilities to support students’ learning experiences.
